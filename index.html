The Memory

It was something special on the Guilford shore

Back in the fall of 2013, Guilford came alive with the sounds, smells, and spirit of a classic Oktoberfest — right along the Connecticut shoreline. Craft beer, live music, local vendors, and the warmth of a town coming together.

the festival drew crowds to the Guilford Fairgrounds on Lover's Lane for three incredible days — October 18, 19, and 20. Jimmy Sturr & His Orchestra, Hofbräuhaus beer, 33,000 sq ft of tented fun, and 100% of proceeds donated to charity.

This campaign is about finding out: are enough of us ready to bring it back?

The smell of bratwurst, the sound of a polka band drifting off the water, families and neighbors all in one place on a crisp October afternoon. That was Guilford at its absolute best.

— A 2013 Attendee

The Team Behind It

The Same People.
The Same Passion.

This isn't a new idea from people who heard about a festival. This is the original team — the people who actually built the 2013 Oktoberfest — coming back to do it again.

10+ Years of
Experience

The Members of Edelweiss Lodge of the Hermann Sons of Connecticut organized and ran the original 2013 Guilford Shoreline Oktoberfest from the ground up. We booked the venue at the Guilford Fairgrounds on Lover's Lane, coordinated entertainment including Jimmy Sturr & His Orchestra, brought in Hofbräuhaus beer from Munich, and delivered a true Oktoberfest experience across three full days. We know exactly what it takes — because we've already done it. Now, as a fully registered 501(c) charitable organization, we're coming back with a clear mission: every dollar raised goes to support local charities.

✅ Proven Event Organizers

The Roadmap

How This Comes Together

A grassroots campaign builds in stages. Here's where we're going.

01

Gauge Community Interest

Collect interest pledges and volunteer sign-ups. Our goal: 1000 signatures that show real, local demand to organizers and the town.

📍 We Are Here
02

Approach Local Businesses & Sponsors

Armed with pledge numbers, approach Guilford-area businesses, breweries, and restaurants about sponsorship.

🎯 Next Step
03

Launch GoFundMe — $50,000 Goal

Open our formal GoFundMe covering permits, venue, entertainment, and logistics — so festival profits go entirely to local charities.

💰 Funding Phase
04

Secure the Guilford Fairgrounds & Date

Lock in Lover's Lane for an October weekend and build out the volunteer team from our sign-up list.

📅 Logistics
05

Guilford Shoreline Oktoberfest Returns 🍺

Cold beer, live music, local food, autumn air, the CT shoreline — and a donation to our local charities.
