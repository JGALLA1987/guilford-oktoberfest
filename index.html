The Memory

It was something special on the Guilford shore

Back in the fall of 2013, Guilford came alive with the sounds, smells, and spirit of a classic Oktoberfest — right along the Connecticut shoreline. Craft beer, live music, local vendors, and the warmth of a town coming together.

It was the kind of event that feels like Guilford at its best. And then it was gone. But the memory stayed.

This campaign is about finding out: are enough of us ready to bring it back?

The smell of bratwurst, the sound of a polka band drifting off the water, families and neighbors all in one place on a crisp October afternoon. That was Guilford at its absolute best.

— A 2013 Attendee

The Team Behind It

The Same People.
The Same Passion.

This isn't a new idea from people who heard about a festival. This is the original team — the people who actually built the 2013 Oktoberfest — coming back to do it again, better than ever.

10+ Years of
Experience

The Edelweiss Lodge of the Hermann Sons of Connecticut organized and ran the original 2013 Guilford Shoreline Oktoberfest from the ground up. We booked the venue, coordinated the vendors, brought in the entertainment, and poured the beer. We know exactly what it takes — because we've already done it. Now, as a fully registered 501(c) charitable organization, we're coming back with even stronger community ties, better planning, and a mission that goes beyond a great party: every dollar raised goes to support our local VFW.

✅ Proven Event Organizers

We ran the 2013 festival successfully — same logistics team, same commitment to quality, zero guesswork on what works.

🏛️ Established 501(c) Nonprofit

The Edelweiss Lodge is a registered charitable organization. Your donation is accountable, transparent, and mission-driven.

🤝 Deep Community Roots

Our Lodge has been part of Connecticut's fabric for generations — we know this community, and this community knows us.

🎯 Clear Charitable Goal

Net proceeds from the first festival go directly to the local VFW, supporting veterans who served this country.

The Roadmap

How This Comes Together

A grassroots campaign builds in stages. Here's where we're going.

01

Gauge Community Interest

Collect interest pledges and volunteer sign-ups through this page and social media. Our goal: 200 signatures that show real, local demand.

📍 We Are Here
02

Approach Local Businesses & Sponsors

Armed with pledge numbers and donation data, approach Guilford-area businesses, breweries, and restaurants about sponsorship.

🎯 Next Step
03

Launch GoFundMe Campaign

Open our formal GoFundMe with a $50,000 goal — covering permits, venue, entertainment, and logistics — so the festival's profits go entirely to the VFW.

💰 Funding Phase
04

Secure the Venue & Date

Work with the Town of Guilford to lock in a waterfront location and an October date. Build out the volunteer team from our sign-up list.

📅 Logistics
05

Guilford Shoreline Oktoberfest Returns 🍺

Cold beer, live music, local food, autumn air, the CT shoreline — and a donation to our local VFW. If the community shows up now, the festival shows up in October.
